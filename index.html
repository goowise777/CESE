Bridging the Gap in Gulf Industrial Supply

For over a decade, Commercial Electricity Supply Establishment (CESE) has served as a specialized distributor of industrial automation controls, sensors, pneumatics, and power transmission products across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the broader GCC.

We streamline procurement for manufacturing, chemical processing, oil & gas installations, and water treatment utilities. By maintaining direct relationships with top-tier international manufacturers, we provide genuine engineering supplies with certified traceability and robust local technical support.
